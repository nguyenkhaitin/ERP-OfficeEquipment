> *A complete business workflow simulation demonstrating the configuration and deployment of an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system.*

This project was developed for the **Enterprise Information Systems** course at the **University of Social Sciences and Humanities (USSH)**. It showcases the practical application of business administration concepts by fully configuring an Odoo 16 environment to manage the manufacturing and trading operations of an office equipment company.

---

### My Role: ERP Implementer & Business Analyst
Unlike traditional coding projects, this system required deep business acumen and system configuration skills. I was responsible for mapping real-world business requirements into the Odoo ecosystem:

* **Business Process Mapping:** Analyzed and designed workflows for manufacturing, inventory, and sales departments to ensure seamless data flow across the enterprise.
* **System Configuration:** Customized Odoo modules (CRM, Sales, Manufacturing) without writing custom code, demonstrating a strong understanding of out-of-the-box ERP capabilities.
* **Marketing Automation & CRM:** Designed automated customer journey pipelines, implementing email marketing campaigns and lead scoring systems to optimize the sales funnel.
* **Master Data Management:** Structured and simulated realistic business data (products, vendors, customer databases) to test the system's operational viability.

---

### Key Objectives
* **Workflow Integration:** Eliminate departmental silos by integrating Sales, CRM, and Manufacturing processes into a centralized system.
* **Process Automation:** Reduce manual workload in marketing and customer follow-ups using Odoo's Automation rules.
* **Systematic Reporting:** Utilize built-in analytics to track sales performance, lead conversion rates, and manufacturing efficiency.

---

### Simulated Business Modules
The project covers a complete business cycle, utilizing the following core Odoo 16 applications:

#### 1. CRM & Sales
* Designed a custom Sales Pipeline to track leads from initial contact to closed deals.
* Configured automated quotation generation and invoicing directly linked to inventory levels.

#### 2. Marketing Automation
* Built automated email workflows triggered by customer actions (e.g., abandoned carts, post-purchase follow-ups).
* Implemented lead nurturing strategies to improve conversion rates.

#### 3. Manufacturing & Inventory
* Simulated the production process for office equipment by defining Bill of Materials (BoM).
* Tracked work orders and automated stock deductions based on manufacturing output.

---

### Technical & Business Stack
* **ERP Platform:** Odoo 16
* **Core Modules:** CRM, Sales, Marketing Automation, Manufacturing (MRP), Inventory.
* **Methodology:** Business Process Modeling, System Configuration, Master Data Management.
* **Documentation:** Microsoft Word (Comprehensive Implementation Report).
